Add two classes to the H1 element, to make the background pink and the color red.

    <!DOCTYPE html>
    <html>
    <head>
    <style>
    .intro {background:pink;}
    .special {color:red;}
    </style>
    </head>
    <body>
    
    <h1 class="intro special">My Home Page</h1>
    
    </body>
    </html>
